# Privacy Policy

Last updated May 12, 2026

World Cup Predictions bot respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains
how we collect, use, disclose, retain, and safeguard information when you use
the bot on Discord. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not
agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use this bot.

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time and for any reason. We will
indicate changes by updating the "Last updated" date above.

By using World Cup Predictions bot on Discord, you also use Discord's
services. Discord's own data practices are governed by Discord's [Privacy
Policy](https://discord.com/privacy).

## Collection of Your Information

We collect only the information needed to run World Cup prediction leagues,
score submitted predictions, support server administration, and operate the bot
reliably.

Information we may collect includes:

- Discord user IDs.
- Discord server IDs.
- Discord channel IDs configured for prediction announcements and
  leaderboards.
- Cached Discord display names or usernames, used to make leaderboards and
  summaries readable.
- Prediction data submitted through the bot, including group rankings,
  predicted third-place qualifiers, knockout picks, champion picks, runner-up
  picks, third-place picks, and related bracket data.
- Prediction history needed to audit submitted picks, edits, and score
  changes.
- Scores, ranks, point breakdowns, and leaderboard records.
- Server-specific bot configuration, such as scoring settings, lock deadlines,
  prediction status, configured channels, and attached tournament data.
- Admin and operator action records, such as setup changes, exports, backups,
  recalculations, sync runs, lock changes, and other actions needed for audit
  and troubleshooting.
- Operational data needed to keep the bot running, such as migration state,
  result sync status, provider cache metadata, result warning records, and
  application logs.
- Command inputs you provide directly to the bot, such as slash command options
  and prediction selections.

Information we do not intentionally collect includes:

- Discord passwords.
- Discord account email addresses.
- Payment card or banking information.
- Government IDs.
- Precise physical location.
- Voice chat audio.
- General server message contents unrelated to direct bot commands or bot
  workflows.
- Sensitive personal information that is not needed for prediction league
  operation.

Do not submit sensitive personal information through the bot.

## Use of Your Information

We use collected information to:

- Run Discord server-specific World Cup prediction leagues.
- Save, edit, lock, score, and display submitted predictions.
- Generate prediction summaries, group images, bracket images, point
  breakdowns, ranks, and leaderboards.
- Apply server-specific scoring rules, lock deadlines, and configuration.
- Sync official tournament results and recalculate scores.
- Provide admin exports and backups for the Discord server where a league is
  running.
- Detect, troubleshoot, audit, and fix operational issues.
- Protect the bot from abuse, spam, unauthorized access, and misuse.
- Respond to support requests and data deletion requests.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your prediction data to
create AI-generated predictions or recommendation systems.

## Prediction Visibility

Prediction entry and edit flows are intended to be private Discord interactions
while you are filling out a bracket. However, once submitted, prediction data is
part of the server's prediction league.

Submitted predictions, champion picks, runner-up picks, third-place picks,
group images, bracket images, scores, point breakdowns, and leaderboard
positions may be visible to other members of the same Discord server through
bot commands, generated images, summaries, and leaderboard posts.

Server administrators may also be able to export or back up server league data,
including predictions, scores, settings, stored results, and audit records.
Server administrators are responsible for handling exported or backed-up data
appropriately after they receive it.

## Disclosure of Your Information

We may disclose information in the following situations:

**Within a Discord server.** Submitted predictions, scores, rankings,
leaderboards, and generated summaries may be displayed to members of the
Discord server where the league is running.

**To server administrators.** Server administrators may access exports,
backups, configuration data, and audit information for leagues in servers they
manage.

**To service providers.** The bot may rely on hosting providers, database
providers, logging infrastructure, Discord, and tournament result providers to
operate. Information may be processed by those services as necessary to provide
the bot. The bot does not intentionally send your submitted prediction data to
tournament result providers.

**By law or to protect rights.** If we believe disclosure is necessary to
respond to legal process, investigate or remedy potential violations of our
policies, protect the rights, property, or safety of others, or comply with
applicable law, we may disclose information as permitted or required by law.

## Third-Party Services

World Cup Predictions bot operates through Discord and may use public or
third-party tournament result data sources. Discord and those services may
collect or process information according to their own policies.

The bot is not responsible for the privacy practices of Discord, hosting
providers, tournament organizers, live result providers, or other third-party
services.

## Retention and Deletion

We retain information for as long as needed to operate prediction leagues,
preserve scoring and audit history, troubleshoot issues, maintain backups,
comply with legal obligations, and prevent abuse.

Server administrators or users may request deletion of data by contacting the
bot operator using the support information shown by the bot's `/help` command
or through the support channel provided for the server. Some information may be
retained when necessary for audit logs, backups, legal compliance, security,
abuse prevention, scoring integrity, or legitimate operational needs.

Removing the bot from a Discord server does not necessarily delete all stored
data automatically. Contact the bot operator for deletion requests.

## Security of Your Information

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to help protect stored
information. These measures may include access controls, environment-based
secret management, database credentials, logging safeguards, and limiting
stored data to information needed for bot operation.

No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure. We cannot
guarantee absolute security of your information.

## Children's Privacy

The bot is intended for use on Discord and is subject to Discord's age and
account requirements. The bot is not intended to knowingly collect personal
information from anyone who is not permitted to use Discord.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, want to learn more about the
data the bot collects, or would like to request deletion of your information,
contact the bot operator using the support information shown by the bot's
`/help` command or through the support channel provided for your server.
